Bio plastics

 Bioplastics are a type of plastic made from renewable resources such as corn starch, sugarcane, and potato starch, rather than non-renewable resources like petroleum. Bioplastics can be composted, which reduces the environmental impact of plastic waste. They also have the potential to reduce greenhouse gas emissions because they are made from renewable resources

Bio plastics

Biofuels are renewable fuels made from living organisms. Algae can be used to produce biofuels because they grow quickly and can be cultivated in large quantities. Algae biofuels have the potential to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and dependence on non-renewable fossil fuels.

Bio constructional material

Construction materials made from renewable resources such as bamboo, straw, and hemp are considered bio constructional raw materials. These materials have the potential to reduce the environmental impact of construction by using renewable resources and reducing waste.

Our Services

1. BIO-PLASTICS

Bioplastics are a type of plastic that is made from renewable, bio-based resources such as corn starch, agar, glycerin, cassava starch, and potato starch.

 

Learn more

2. BIO-FUEL

Biofuels are renewable fuels that are derived from biological materials such as crops, waste, and algae. Algae are a promising source of biofuels due to their high productivity, fast growth rate, and ability to grow in a wide range of conditions. 


Learn more

3. BIO-CONSTRUCTIONAL MATERIAL

Collecting carbon emissions from industries and vehicles and using them to create innovative and sustainable building materials is one of our sustainable innovations. The collected carbon is processed and transformed into carbon tiles, carbon bricks, and carbon rooftop tiles, which can be used in construction projects.
